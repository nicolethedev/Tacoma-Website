Why We Love Tacoma

Waterfront

With a 5-mile stretch of waterfront, Tacoma offers a variety of activities for all ages. From kayaking to paddleboarding, there is something for everyone.

Museum of Glass

Located on the Thea Foss Waterway, the Museum of Glass is a must-see for art lovers. Watch live glass blowing demonstrations and explore the galleries.

Farmers Market

Every Thursday, the Broadway Farmers Market offers fresh produce, flowers, and artisan goods. Support local farmers and businesses while enjoying the outdoors.
